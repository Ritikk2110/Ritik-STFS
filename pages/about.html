About Smart Things Finder System
Smart Things Finder System is a digital platform designed to help individuals quickly and efficiently report, search, and recover lost and found items within a campus or institution.
Our mission is simple:
To bridge the gap between people who lose things and those who find them—through technology, trust, and teamwork.

In most institutions, the lost and found process is outdated, manual, and often inefficient. We built this system to modernize and simplify that experience. Instead of checking notice boards or asking around, students and staff can now use our platform to instantly log reports and find matches for lost and found items.

Key Features :
1.Easy-to-use interface for all users
2.Searchable item database
3.Photo uploads for better item recognition
4.Notifications for potential matches
5.Admin panel for verifying and resolving disputes
6.Works on all devices—PCs, mobiles, and tablets
